2. HOW TO ADD A NEW CAR FROM YOUR PHONE

Step 1: Upload Car Photo
  Go to GitHub > "premium-cars" > "Add file" > "Upload files"
  Upload your car image. Example: mercedes-gle-2024.jpeg

Step 2: Open Admin Panel
  Go to: /admin.html
  Login with password above

Step 3: Fill Car Details
  Car ID: mercedes-gle-2024        <- lowercase, no spaces
  Car Name: MERCEDES GLE 2024
  Description: Clean and Accident Free
  Status: New or Used
  Origin: Foreign or Local
  Image Filename: mercedes-gle-2024.jpeg
  Featured: Yes or No

Step 4: Click "Generate Car Code" > "Copy Car Code"

Step 5: Go to GitHub > "script.js" > Click pencil ✏️
  Paste the code at the end of the cars list
  Click "Commit changes"

Step 6: Refresh your website. Done!
